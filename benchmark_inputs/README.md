# Benchmark Inputs

Place your test audio files in this folder before running the benchmark script.

Use at least three files:

- `short.wav`: 5 to 10 seconds
- `medium.wav`: 15 to 30 seconds
- `large.wav`: 45 to 60 seconds

Suggested content:

- Short: one sentence
- Medium: one short paragraph
- Large: two to three paragraphs

If you use formats other than `.wav`, the benchmark still works, but you should write the duration manually in your report.
